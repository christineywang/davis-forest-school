About

Davis Forest School is an outdoor education organization committed to guiding the next generation of children to wholeness and connectedness to nature, their communities, and ultimately themselves.

In our outdoor programs, children and their families develop personal experiences with nature. From the quiet of trickling water to the buzz of birds chirping, children engage with the changing dynamics of their natural surroundings and experience the ultimate in imaginative, exploratory play.
