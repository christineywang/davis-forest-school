About

Davis Forest School is an outdoor education organization committed to guiding the next generation of children to wholeness and connectedness to nature, their communities, and ultimately themselves.

In our outdoor programs, children and their families develop first-hand experiences with nature. From the quiet of trickling water to the buzz of birds chirping, children engage with the changing dynamics of their natural surroundings and experience the ultimate in imaginative, exploratory play. Through these experiences, it is our hope that our community begins to understand the interconnection between all living beings and the reciprocal relationship that humans have with both the natural world and each other.
